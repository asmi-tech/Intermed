FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and appointments.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at (253) 456-1189, using our online contact form, or visiting our office directly. We'll work with you to find a convenient time.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance cards, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from other healthcare providers.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office to verify coverage for your specific plan before your appointment.

What are your office hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.
